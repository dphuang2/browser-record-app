Rewind Frequently Asked Questions

  • What makes Rewind different from other recording apps?

    •
    1. Rewind is extremely cheap compared to similar alternatives. We offer a transparent pricing plan with no additional bells and whistles that you have to pay for. Everything is included.
    2. Rewind values privacy. We only collect the necessary information that will allow Rewind to function. We do not track or identify any customers and merchants.
    3. Rewind is high quality. Our application is meticulously engineered to give you high quality playback functionality, a quick and snappy user experience, and a well-designed user interface at an extremely affordable price.
    4. Rewind is easy to install and uninstall. Rewind only installs a single script tag to your shop. This script tag is automatically removed when you remove Rewind from your shop. No need for theme modifications or additional setup steps. Just click install and you are good to go!

  • How do I install Rewind?

    •  No additional setup is needed after installing Rewind through the app store.

  • Is Rewind GDPR compliant?

    • Yes. Rewind does not store any identifiable information so it is completely GDPR compliant.

  • How many customer replays can I store per month?

    • Unlimited! Rewind is highly engineered to bring you a powerful yet cheap analytical tool.

  • Will Rewind leave bloat in my website?

    • The only thing that Rewind installs is a script tag. Script tags are automatically uninstalled with the app so no trace is left behind.

  • Does Rewind record passwords from customers?

    • No. Rewind censors all inputs of type "password".

  • What happens to my shop's replays when I uninstall Rewind?

    •  We do not delete any replays from our database. If you would like to delete replays from our database, please contact rewind.the.app@gmail.com.

  • How do I contact Rewind?
