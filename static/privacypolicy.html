Loopr Privacy Policy

Privacy is extremely valuable and we deeply understand privacy concerns in our modern digital world.

Loopr does not store any personally identifiable information.

The only customer data that Loopr needs to store is information regarding customer interactions such as mouse movement, mouse clicks, page loads, and page contents. Each replay is tagged with an 100% anonymous ID.

The only thing that Loopr stores for each merchant is their shop name and recurring charge ID. The only permissions we request are access to read and write script tags.
