Rewind Privacy Policy

Privacy is extremely valuable and we deeply understand privacy concerns in our modern digital world.

Rewind does not store any personally identifiable information in its databases. All persisted data is impossible to link to an identity.

The only customer data that Rewind needs to store is information regarding customer interactions such as mouse movement, mouse clicks, page loads, and page contents. Each visiting customer is tagged with an 100% anonymous ID

The only thing that Rewind stores for each merchant is their shop name and recurring charge ID. The only permissions we request are access to read and write script tags.
