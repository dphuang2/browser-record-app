Rewind Privacy Policy

Privacy is extremely valuable and we deeply understand privacy concerns in our modern digital world.

Rewind does not store any personally identifiable information in its databases. All persisted data is impossible to link to an identity.

The only customer data that Rewind needs to store is serialized information regarding customer interactions such as mouse movement, mouse clicks, page loads, and page contents. To link customers and their interaction data, each customer is tagged with a 100% anonymous ID.

The only thing that Rewind stores for each merchant is their shop name and recurring charge id. And the only permissions we request are access to read and write script tags.
